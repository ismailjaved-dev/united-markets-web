ABOUT US

Why United Markets?

We’re a fintech trading company founded in 2016 by Scandinavian entrepreneurs, with a track record of success. With a rapidly growing international presence, and offices in Cyprus, Malta and Spain, we provide access to over 1,000 of the world’s top financial markets.

United Markets enables you to trade CFD instruments across the most renowned asset classes across multiple award-winning platforms, including its own advanced proprietary platform.
Its competitive pricing, reliability and quick execution, as well as our client-centric focus, are the reasons why more and more traders are switching to United Market.
As we continue to expand and surpass our goals, United Market's vision remains simple: to make investment and trading engaging and accessible to everyone, in a transparent and secure way.

Our vision

United Market’s vision is simple: to unlock the potential of the world’s markets.

We believe that trading is for everyone, and anyone with an interest in financial markets should have the opportunity to trade to their full potential.

We want to make trading easily accessible to all in a transparent and trusted environment.

Our mission

We’ve been on a mission to create our own unique trading platform, designed to cater to traders of all levels. Our proprietary platform United Markets Trader now connects everyone to the world’s financial markets, and we continue to expand our horizons as we follow trading trends.

As we continuously search for new ways to push the boundaries of technology, we work tirelessly on offering our clients more trading options and better trading conditions. We thrive by truly understanding our customers’ needs, with a long-term mission to develop the best products and services within the fintech industry. 

Safe in our hands

United Markets is a regulated broker with strict measures in place to adhere to full transparency. Our headquarters are in Cyprus, in Europe: United Markets Ltd is authorised and regulated by the Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission (CySEC) under CIF licence No. 357/18. United Markets (Seychelles) Ltd, is authorised and regulated by the Financial Services Authority (FSA) under licence No. SD042.
