About the competition

What are the dates for the competition?

The competition will take place each Sunday 8am UTC to 8am UTC Monday. Make sure to mark your calendar and boost your chances for a bigger prize.

How can I increase my chances of winning?

To have a shot at winning, focus on executing profitable trades while managing risk effectively. Keep an eye on market trends, study trading strategies, and make informed decisions based on market analysis.Traders who generate the highest net PnL with trades that generate more than $10 in markups and commissions will become the winners.
For more information, check our T&C.

How do I identify myself on the leaderboard?

To identify yourself on the leaderboard, follow these steps:

1. Look for your ID on the leaderboard. It will include the last 4 digits of your ID.

2. To find your personal ID, check the special email we send on the day of the competition.

3. Additionally, ensure that you have enabled promotional communications to receive important updates and information.

What are the rewards for the competition?

We're presenting a total prize pool of $10,000 for this competition, with rewards distributed among five winners. The first-place winner will receive $5000, the second-place winner will earn $4000, and the remaining three winners will receive $500, $300, and $200, respectively.

How do you calculate who wins?

To be eligible for winning, participants’ positions must generate more than $10 in markups and commissions. All positions with these fees will be included in the leaderboard, increasing your chances of winning. To determine your success, we use a formula called ROI. To find your ROI, multiply your total trade costs (including fees and expenses) by your trading profit and loss (PnL) and divide that by the total amount of money you've deposited into your trading account over your trading journey. If you've generated the highest ROI during the competition, you'll be the winner.
