  • Tier 1: $2 per lot (0M-10M trading volume traded)
  • Tier 2: $5 per lot (10M-150M trading volume traded)

Cashback rebates are paid per round turn lot (1 Lot = 100,000 units in trading volume).

Your cashback is calculated by multiplying your closed traded volume with the corresponding rebate level. The rebate level corresponds to the tier that the volume of the specific trade has reached.
Your traded volume is equivalent to the notional value of your CFD position which varies according to the contract size and price of the instrument you are trading.

How do I get the cashback?

+ -

All you need to do to qualify for the cashback is register a live account with Skilling, verify your account and mantain a net deposit of $100. The cashback is automatically enabled to your account and is credited and sent automatically at the closing of each eligible trade, which you complete within any given month.

What’s notional value?

+ -

A CFD’s notional value is the contract value for one CFD. It is the financial expression of the CFD and it is calculated by multiplying the CFD contract size (units) by the CFD price.

On United Markets Trader and cTrader you use a unit structure to compute the contract size. Whereas on MetaTrader4 you use a lot structure where 100,000 units is equal to one lot for forex pairs. Units per 1 lot vary on non-forex pairs.

How is the cashback calculated?

+ -

Your cashback is calculated by multiplying your closed traded volume with the corresponding rebate level. The rebate level corresponds to the tier that the volume of the specific trade has reached.

Your traded volume is equivalent to the notional value of your CFD position which varies according to the contract size (number of units traded) and price of the instrument you are trading. Keep in mind that trading with leverage increases your exposure in the markets.You can trade the same amount of units with less capital.

For example, you trade EURUSD and you reach 5M in notional volume traded over the course of the month. Your trade volume is within Tier 1 and you will receive $2 per lot traded. Your total rebate amount is $50.

In another example, you reach 200M in notional volume over the course of the month. Your trade volume is split into 10M in Tier 1, and 190M in Tier 2. Your total rebate amount is $4850:(10*10)+(190*25).

Please note that the cashback is computed on a USD basis and is based on the aggregate volume traded across all your accounts with Skilling. Currency exchange rates and fees apply when trading accounts are not denominated in USD so final cashback amounts will vary.

Do I need a specific United Markets account?

+ -

No, you can claim your cashback by trading on any of Skilling’s trading accounts (TradingView, United Markets Trader, MetaTrader 4 or cTrader). The cashback is automatically enabled when you create your account and you will start earning your cash back rebates as soon as you are eligible to receive them - when you are verified and you have a net deposit of $100 or more.

I have multiple accounts with Skilling. Is cashback limited to only one trading account?

+ -

No, the United Markets Cashback Program is rewarded per customer profile which means all your accounts are included in the cashback calculation. If you have multiple trading accounts, the total notional value of the month will be the sum of how much was traded on each account. The monthly lump sum cashback payment will be paid into each account respectively. Any accounts not denominated in USD will be converted into the respective currency (exchange rate and fees apply).

Are there any restrictions for the cashback?

+ -

There are no restrictions with regards to instruments you can trade. All instruments are included in the program. Trades simply need to be open for 3 or more minutes to be eligible for the cashback rebate. The maximum cashback total rebate you can get in the course of a month is $10,000.

Clients must however maintain eligibility in order to get the cashback at the end of the month — you must have a net deposit equal or greater than $100. Cashback is not calculated on a pro-rata basis.

What instruments are eligible to receive cashback?

+ -

All instruments on United Markets Trader, MetaTrader 4, cTrader are included in the cashback rebate program. Notional value per instrument varies according to the contract size and price of each instrument at the time of trading.

When do I receive the cashback?

+ -

You receive the cashback you earn during the month as a lump sum on the first day of the next month. The cashback is calculated based on the aggregate value across all your accounts and will be credited proportionally to each account.

Do I get a cashback if a trade is closed with a loss?

+ -

Yes, the cashback is earned irrespective of whether your trade closes with a profit or a loss.
